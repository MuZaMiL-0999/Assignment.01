Chicken

Biryani

Recipe Card

A rich and aromatic rice dish layered with spiced chicken, saffron, and fragrant herbs. A classic from Indian cuisine that's perfect for special occasions or a flavorful meal.

Prep Time:30 mins
Servings: 4

Ingredients

  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 500g chicken (bone-in preferred)
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 2 large onions, sliced
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • Whole spices (bay leaves, cinnamon, cloves, cardamom)
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • A few saffron strands soaked in warm milk
  • Fresh coriander and mint leaves
  • Salt and oil/ghee

Steps

  1. Wash and soak rice for 30 minutes. Cook until 70% done and set aside.
  2. Marinate chicken with yogurt, spices, and ginger-garlic paste. Rest for 30 minutes.
  3. Fry onions until golden brown. Remove half for garnish.
  4. Cook marinated chicken with tomatoes, fried onions, and spices until tender.
  5. In a pot, layer chicken, rice, saffron milk, mint, coriander, and fried onions.
  6. Cover tightly and cook on low heat (dum) for 20-25 minutes.
  7. Fluff gently before serving. Serve hot with raita or salad.
